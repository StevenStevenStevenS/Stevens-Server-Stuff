Primary

Primary is a game about living displaced in a small town. It's art relates to the main character's home's structures, and how he views them.
This was of personal interest to me, because I'm from a small town and I've never been able to find a plesant narrative for it. My art from this game, I hope, reflects, this experimentation with how I view my home.
